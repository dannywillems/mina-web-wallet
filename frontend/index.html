Mina Web Wallet

Generate and manage Mina wallets securely in your browser.
All cryptographic operations happen locally - your keys never leave your device.

Supports hex (64 chars) or Base58 (52 chars) format
Security Notice: This wallet runs entirely in your browser. Your private keys are never sent to any server. However, always ensure you're using this tool on a secure, private device. Never share your secret keys with anyone.
